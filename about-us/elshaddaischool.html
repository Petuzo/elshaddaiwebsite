About Us

El-Shaddai Primary School is a faith-based institution committed to providing a high-quality education rooted in Christian values. We strive to cultivate a nurturing environment where students not only excel academically but also develop strong moral character, integrity, and a sense of purpose. Our mission is to inspire young minds to embrace lifelong learning while instilling in them a deep respect for Christian principles, discipline, and compassion. Through a balanced approach to education, we aim to shape responsible and confident individuals who will positively impact their communities and the world.
