El-Shaddai Primary School - Visiting Day

Visiting Day at 2024

A special day filled with love, joy, and connection! Visiting Day at El-Shaddai Primary School is a cherished moment where parents and guardians get the opportunity to spend quality time with their children, witness their academic and personal growth, and engage with teachers to discuss progress. It’s a time to strengthen the bond between home and school, celebrate achievements, and create beautiful memories that will last a lifetime. We look forward to welcoming our beloved families for a day of warmth, encouragement, and togetherness!.
