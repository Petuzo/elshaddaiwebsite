Frequently Asked Questions

1. What curriculum does El-Shaddai Primary School follow?

El-Shaddai Primary School follows the Tanzanian national curriculum with a focus on academic excellence and Christian values.

2. What are the school hours?

Classes run from Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM.

3. How do I apply for admission?

Parents can visit our school office or contact us via email to get the admission forms and further details.

4. Does the school provide meals?

Yes, the school provides nutritious meals for students during lunch hours.

5. Are there extracurricular activities?

Yes, we offer sports, music, arts, and other extracurricular activities to enhance student development.
